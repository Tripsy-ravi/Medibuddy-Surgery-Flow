1
Chapter 1
A Patient Arrives
Someone contacts MediBuddy looking for surgery help. An opportunity is created in the CRM system.
🖥️ System — CRM
Opportunity is Created
A new case is opened for the patient. At this stage, only basic info is captured:
📱 Contact Number 👤 Name 🏥 Source (where they came from) 🏢 Department (for limited cases)
Next step
📋 CareBuddy Team picks it up
CB Team Is Assigned
A CareBuddy (CB) agent is assigned to this case. Their job: call the patient, understand their need, and qualify them for surgery through MediBuddy's network.
⏱️ Auto-Lost Rule: If no action is taken for 15 days at any stage, the lead is automatically marked as "Lost."
2
Chapter 2
The First Call
The CB agent calls the patient. What happens next depends on whether the patient picks up — and what they say.
📞 CareBuddy Team
CB Agent Calls the Patient
The agent dials the patient for the first time and tries to reach them.
⚡ Did the patient answer?
📵 Not Connected
Marked as "Not Connected / RNR"
RNR = Ring No Response. CB will retry up to 3 times. If still no answer after 3 tries, the case pauses for later follow-up.
✅ Connected
CB Gets Intent & Details
The agent talks to the patient to understand what surgery they need and whether MediBuddy can help.
If connected
⚡ Is the patient interested in proceeding?
❌ Not Interested
Case Saved & Closed
Sub-disposition captured: Not Interested, City not serviceable, Network/surgery not available, Casual browsing, etc. Basic details saved and form closed.
✅ Interested
Qualified — Case Moves Forward
Patient is marked as Qualified = Yes with a timestamp. CB now captures deeper medical details.
🏥 Hospital preference 👨‍⚕️ Doctor preference 📍 Location & Map Link 🏢 Sub Department 📅 OPD Requested Date 🏙️ City, Pin Code 👤 Gender, Relationship
✉️ OPD Status → "Yet to be Scheduled"
If qualified
⚡ Is this an MB Internal case?
❌ MB Internal = No
Move to Partner
Case is transferred to a partner hospital outside MediBuddy's direct network. A reason is captured for the transfer.
✅ MB Internal = Yes
Stays within MediBuddy
Case continues through MediBuddy's own network and scheduling system. Moves to OPD booking.
⚡ Special Rule
Surgery / City Not Serviceable?
If the sub-disposition indicates the patient's city or surgery type cannot be serviced, an automatic email is sent to the Network Team, TL, and CB with basic patient details.
✉️ Email → Network Team, TL, CB ✉️ Email → Yarab/Fasi (Name, Sub-Dept, Contact, City, Link)
3
Chapter 3
Booking the OPD Appointment
OPD = Out-Patient Department — this is the initial doctor consultation before surgery. The CST (Scheduling) team now takes over to confirm a slot with the hospital.
📅 CST / Scheduling Team
CST Team Gets Notified
The CST team sees this case in their Smart View and contacts the hospital to find an available slot for the patient's OPD visit.
⚡ Did the hospital confirm a slot?
❌ Slot Not Confirmed
Marked as Hospital / Customer Dependency
CST marks OPD Status = "Hospital Dependency" or "Customer Dependency" with a reason.
✉️ Email → CB (Reason + Slot failed)
🔁 CB re-checks with patient and places a new OPD Requested Date with Status = "Future Dated / Reschedule"
✅ Slot Confirmed
OPD Status = "OPD Fixed"
CST marks the slot as confirmed, adds OPD Confirmed Date. If original doctor isn't available, an external doctor name goes in a separate field.
✉️ Email → Hospital & Patient (OPD Details) 💬 WhatsApp → Patient (OPD Details)
Once OPD Fixed
⏰ Automatic Reminders
Reminder Emails Sent Before OPD
Two automatic reminder emails go out to keep everyone prepared:
✉️ Evening before OPD date ✉️ Morning of OPD date
4
Chapter 4
The OPD Visit
The patient visits the doctor. Several outcomes are possible — and each one triggers a different path.
📅 Day of OPD
OPD Status is Updated
After the OPD visit (or non-visit), the CB team updates the OPD Status to reflect what happened. Several automatic follow-ups also fire during the day:
💬 WhatsApp to patient 2 hrs after OPD (if Attended) 🔔 IVR call — 3 attempts (if still OPD Fixed status) ✉️ Evening feedback email 💬 Evening WhatsApp for status update
If patient replies to IVR or WhatsApp, the status is auto-updated.
⚡ What happened at the OPD? (Pick the outcome)
✅ Attended
Patient visited the doctor
CB must now fill 3 mandatory fields:
• Decoding Request (Yes/No)
• Post OPD Status
• Prescription Status + Upload
⏸ No Show
Patient didn't arrive
Sub-reason captured. Case is assessed for reschedule or closure.
🔄 Reschedule
New date needed
Sub-reason captured. A new OPD Requested Date is set.
❌ Cancelled by Hospital
Hospital cancelled
Sub-reason captured. Cancellation email sent to hospital and patient.
✉️ Cancellation email → Hospital & Patient
❌ Cancelled by Patient
Patient cancelled
Sub-reason captured. Cancellation email sent to hospital and patient.
✉️ Cancellation email → Hospital & Patient
If Patient Attended → Prescription Flow
⚡ Was a prescription uploaded?
📄 Not Uploaded
CB reminded to upload
Email sent to CB asking them to upload the prescription.
✉️ Email → CB: Please upload prescription
✅ Uploaded
Clinical Coordinator Verifies
The CC team reviews the prescription and marks it as Valid, Partial, or Invalid.
CC reviews prescription
✅ Valid
Success email to CB.
✉️ → CB
⚠️ Partial
CB asked to upload full document.
✉️ → CB
❌ Invalid
CB asked to re-upload correct file.
✉️ → CB
🔄 CB Re-uploads
Document updated → email sent back to CC team for re-review.
✉️ → CC Team
5
Chapter 5
Post OPD, Decoding & Pre-Auth
After the doctor visit, we find out if surgery is needed — and if insurance needs to be arranged first. FinOps and CB work together here.
📋 CareBuddy Team
CB Sets Post OPD Status
Based on what the doctor said, CB selects one of these outcomes:
🧪 Tests Required
Name of test and test date captured. Surgery decision pending results.
💊 Medical Management
No surgery right now — patient needs medication or surgery later. Sub-reasons and timelines captured.
🏥 Surgery Required / Urgent Admission
Surgery needed! IPD Requested Date and Cab Required fields appear. This is the main path forward.
If Surgery Required → Decoding Flow
⚡ Decoding Request — Did the patient request insurance decoding?
❌ Decoding = No
Alert sent to CB
Email and notification reminds CB to recheck — decoding is important for insurance claims.
✉️ Email + 🔔 Notification → CB
✅ Decoding = Yes
FinOps Team Reviews Documents
FinOps sees this in their Smart View and checks if all documents are in order.
FinOps reviews
⚡ Are all documents available?
❌ Documents Missing
Decoding Shared = No
CB is notified to check and gather missing documents before decoding can proceed.
✉️ + 🔔 → CB: Check Documents
✅ All Docs Available
Decoding Shared = Yes
CB is notified to proceed with Pre-Authorization (insurance approval) request.
✉️ + 🔔 → CB: Proceed for PreAuth
PreAuth Request option appears in CB section
⚡ Does the patient want Pre-Authorization (insurance approval)?
❌ PreAuth = No
Reason Captured, Moves to Surgery Scheduling
Patient proceeds without insurance. CB must fill in Tentative Admission Date.
✅ PreAuth = Yes
FinOps Handles PreAuth
FinOps team takes over to file and process the insurance pre-authorization with the TPA (Third Party Administrator).
If PreAuth = Yes
⚡ What is the PreAuth status?
❌ Rejected
PreAuth Rejected
Email sent to CB with rejection reason. CB discusses options with patient.
✉️ Email → CB (Rejection + Reason)
📋 Pending Docs
Documents Needed from CB
CB is asked to upload or modify required insurance documents.
✉️ Email → CB (Action on Docs)
📤 Filed / Under Process
PreAuth Filed with Hospital / TPA
Waiting for TPA response. CB gets update email. If surgery is 3+ days away, a patience email is sent.
✉️ Update → CB & Patient ✉️ "Have patience" email if surgery >3 days away
✅ Approved
PreAuth Approved!
CB and patient are notified. IPD Requested Date and Cab Required fields appear in CB section. Surgery can now be scheduled.
✉️ Approval email → CB & Patient ✉️ Reminder evening before surgery ✉️ Reminder morning of surgery
6
Chapter 6
Surgery, Admission & Discharge
The final stretch — scheduling the surgery, patient admission, the procedure itself, and post-surgery care.
🏥 Surgery Scheduling
Surgery Status = "Yet to Schedule"
CB fills the IPD (In-Patient Department) Requested Date. This triggers the CST team to confirm the actual surgery date with the hospital.
📅 CST Team
CST Confirms IPD Date
Surgery Status → "IPD Scheduled" with a nested status: Future Dated, Back Dated, or Same Day.
✉️ IPD Email → Hospital, Clinical Team & CB
⚡ Does the surgery go ahead as planned?
🔄 Rescheduled by Hospital
Hospital changed the date
Reasons: Lens Unavailable, Room Issues, Doc Unavailable, Equipment/OT unavailable, Patient Denied Advance, Hospital not planned surgery.
✉️ Internal email → CB, TLs, Clinical Coordinators, CST
🔄 Rescheduled by Patient
Patient changed the date
Reasons: Not Available, Not Ready, Cash Issues, Vitals Out of Range, Second Opinion, Health Issues, Fitness Pending, etc.
✉️ Internal email → CB, TLs, Clinical Coordinators, CST
❌ Surgery Cancelled
Surgery will not happen
PreAuth status goes blank. Case is reviewed for closure or alternate path.
✅ Surgery Proceeds
Patient is Admitted
ClinOps team is notified in their view. They update Admission Status and Patient Admission Date.
✉️ Email → Patient (All details) CC: CST, CB
Surgery complete
🏥 FinOps Team
Discharge & Settlement
FinOps receives the case and fills in all financial details:
📅 Discharge Date 💰 Settlement Amount 🧾 Bill Amount
✉️ Email → Hospital (Final amounts)
📱 ClinOps + System
Order Created & Thank You
ClinOps enters Discharge Date. The system creates an order in the MediBuddy app and sends a thank you email to the patient.
✉️ Thank You email → Patient 💬 Feedback + Play Store / App Store link → Patient
🔔 Post-Surgery Follow-Up (Automated)
7-Day Follow-Up Program
Two follow-up tasks are automatically created for the Clinical Coordinators:
📅 Day 3 — Follow-up call with patient 📅 Day 7 — Follow-up call with patient
💬 Day 3 WhatsApp message sent automatically 💬 Day 7 WhatsApp message sent automatically ⚡ Status auto-updates to "Follow-up Provided" regardless of patient reply
🎉
Journey Complete!
The patient has successfully undergone surgery through MediBuddy's network. The case is fully closed with all follow-ups completed.
📌 Note (P0 Priority Cases Only): For high-priority cases, an internal email with full reschedule reasons goes to CB, TLs, Clinical Coordinators, and the CST team upon any reschedule event.
⏱️ Global Auto-Lost Rule
At every stage of this journey, if a lead has had no action for 15 days, the system automatically marks it as Lost. This applies from the very first call stage all the way to the post-surgery stage. Additionally, for P0 cases specifically, if a case is in "OPD Fixed" status and 7 days pass without update, it is also marked as Lost.
